Pet Rock of the Day: Agatha!

Agatha comes to us from Springfield, MO from the richy rich neighborhoods. Agatha is a prim and proper “designer” rock that is used to lounging on bookshelves, being spoiled, and looking pretty. This beautiful girl will be most at home in large houses with owners who will smother her in lots of attention. Don’t be surprised at first if she seems snobby, that is just Agatha’s champagne personality. You’ll soon find out that her personality has many layers.
