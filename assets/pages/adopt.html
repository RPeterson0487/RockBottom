Adoptions

Many are surprised to find that adopting a pet is more work than anticipated. At the Rock Bottom Adoption Agency, it’s our mission to match our pets with the best possible forever home possible. While we reserve the right to refuse any adoption, we will make every effort possible to work with potential adopters to help them find their perfect match.

Please don’t be discouraged if you don’t find the pet you are looking for on your first visit to our page. Adoptable pets are always coming in and out of our shelter, so please keep checking our website regularly to find your next lifelong friend.

Process

Below, you will find a list of our currently available pets with some basic information. Please click on one to go to their page for more information. If you feel you would like to meet what could potentially be your future non-furry family member, please note the name of the pet and click “Contact us for more information” to be taken to an application form. Please fill out this form and click “Submit” and one our staff will be in contact with you to verify your information and likely set up an appointment. We have applications coming in daily, so please allow time for us to process your application.
