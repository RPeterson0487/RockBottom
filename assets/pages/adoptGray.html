Meet Gray

  • Pet ID: 664388
  • Species: Rock
  • Age: 16,87 years, 9 months
  • Size: Large
  • Color: Crème / Black

Gray is our other large breed, though considerably younger. Gray came to us from a quarry in Fulton, MO where he spent his days just laying around. Like other large breeds, he is heavier than some might think, but that doesn’t stop Gray from enjoying his time just sitting in your lap. Unfortunately, he is fairly scarred up from a previous home, so it may take some time to gain Gray’s trust. Once you do, though, he will be the best bud you could hope for. But don’t be surprised to find that Gray can sometimes be a bit hard headed.
